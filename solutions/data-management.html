A Single Source of Truth

When working with numerous data sources, it can be difficult to access accurate, up-to-date information. Our data storage solutions centralize your data sources to enable easy access and collaboration. We offer a range of on-premises, cloud-based, and hybrid data storage solutions, tailored to your org’s unique needs. Our data storage expertise covers: ETL packages, data marts, OLAP cubes, Azure Data Factory (ADF), Blob storage, and Azure Data Lake Storage (ADLS)

Multi-level Security for Sensitive Data

It is more important now than ever to secure your data without sacrificing the ability to effectively collaborate. Our data security solutions offer role-based and row-level access, with security and privacy trainings to ensure your team is up to date on industry best practice. We implement multi-level security checks and disk encryption to secure your information every step of the way. All our solutions are GDPR and SSPA compliant, and follow ISO 27001:2013.

Real-time Data at Your Fingertips

Just because you’re working with big data, doesn’t mean you should be working with big wait times. Our data processing solutions specialize in complex systems with dozens of sources, hundreds of metrics, and thousands of global users. We reduce typical big data processing time from hours to minutes. Our specialties include: Azure Databricks, Azure Synapse, and just-in-time (JIT) processing frameworks.

A Consistent, Accurate Data Pipeline

With enterprise-scale systems, it can be easy to end up with old, unused, or duplicate data. Our data validation solutions combine data validation best practices, an in-house data validation framework, and automated data validation tools to ensure your data remains complete, secure, and accurate. Our solutions include monitoring and optimizing data flows, verifying using A/B and multivariate testing, and optimizing your information architecture with user surveys, site assessments, and UX analysis.

Insights at a Glance

Slow, inaccurate BI can delay key decisions for weeks. Our end-to-end data solutions optimize your data pipeline right up to reporting and analytics. We specialize in enterprise-grade solutions, with over 50,000 senior executives relying on our dashboards every day. Our reporting and analytics solutions enable you to transform your data into insights with competitive intelligence analysis, segmented and pattern user behaviors, real-time Power BI reports, multi-campaign/multi-channel attribution analytics, predictive analytics, and clear, actionable metrics.
