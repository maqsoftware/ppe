Use Dr. Power BI to quickly analyze and fix your Power BI report issues. Get suggestions related to Alignment, Formatting and other visual recommendations. Dr. Power BI provides you an in-depth overview of every report page and its visuals in details to reduce manual effort.

Power BI, a data visualization offering from Microsoft, aims to provide best in industry interactive visualizations and business intelligence capabilities with the tooling simple enough for power users to create their own reports and dashboards.

With the ease of use and high adoption come challenges with ensuring alignment of all reports to the best practices and standards defined by the enterprise. Dr. Power BI tool helps end users quickly review if the reports they have built aligns with corporate standards and if they provide a great user experience.
