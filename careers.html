A little about us

At MAQ Software, we exist to build great software. With expertise in Microsoft technologies, our teams deliver solutions in data visualization, ML/AI, modern apps, and more to Fortune 500 and Global 2000 customers. Our core values in continuous learning, continuous delivery, and continuous improvement means we are always on the hunt for new ideas and technologies that offer previously unknown opportunities.

We are Agile

As an Agile company, we believe in daily delivery and continuous improvement. Results happen fast.

We are always learning

We operate with a learn-it-first mindset and encourage our teams to regularly attend industry-relevant courses and seminars.

We work with the latest technologies

Our teams work with a varied mix of the latest technologies. The work is exciting and challenging.

We are a flat org

We empower team members to take ownership and accountability from day one. You'll be exposed to all parts of project management.

  • Program Manager

    Seeking an experienced and highly motivated project manager.

    Our ideal candidate has broad experience in e-commerce based and/or smart client products, solid technical skills, excellent communication skills, and a track record of shipping great products. Not only will this position require taking on a critical role on a high-performance team but you will help to develop an industry altering product with over a million hits a day. In the workday, you will primarily:

    • Manage and work closely with the development and test teams to help define requirements and feature sets
    • Analyze customer data, gather feedback, and ensure the product meets user requirements
    • Support multiple release schedules throughout the project while handling all PM daily tasks

    Candidate Profile:

    • 3 to 5 years as a Project Manager with strong leadership skills
    • Bachelor's or equivalent degree in related field desired
    • Strong understanding of Project Management and the ability to handle multiple projects and challenges
    • Strong technical background in .NET and Microsoft platform with the ability to develop specifications and partner with development and test teams
    • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, with an ability to communicate difficult technical and business concepts to both technical and non-technical users
    • Previous Microsoft experience is a plus
