  • Sales Coordinator

    This is not another dull, unchallenging administration role where all you do is redirect calls and make coffee. You’ll apply your organizational ability to coordinate the calendars of our Evangelist team, and ensure they’re kept at full utilization with a steady stream of sales meetings.

    Your primary responsibilities will be to:

    • Manage our Evangelist’s calendars. We need you to schedule and coordinate the numerous activities, meetings, and follow-up associated with key sales opportunities that require their involvement.
    • Manage flow of transaction for our Evangelists and Project Leaders from opportunity to close, and conduct post-sale debriefings to manage and improve client relationships.
    • Enter call and appointment outcome data into our CRM to assist opportunity management and reporting.

    The people you’ll be speaking with all have a genuine interest in our services. Often they’ll be existing clients. This is not a sales role — that’s the job of the Evangelist Team! Rather you’ll be setting appointments for them, and coordinating their various activities to ensure every sales opportunity is effectively managed.

    Qualifications and Experience

    Above all else, you will need to be experienced scheduling a busy executive-level calendar and liaising with senior executives and busy decision makers. While your smarts is definitely more important than direct industry experience, preference will be given to candidates who have worked in the software or tech industry in a sales or channel management administration role.

    Other favorable traits include:

    • You’re professional, self-confident and comfortable on the phone.
    • You have coordination experience and are totally at ease communicating with CEOs and the like.
    • You’re organized and have excellent attention to detail.
    • You have MS Office and CRM experience. We use Microsoft Dynamics.

    Benefits

    • A competitive salary.
    • Fidelity 401(k) plan (employer match of 50% up to 6% of your salary) and immediate vesting.
    • A health and dental package.
    • Paid Time Off (PTO)—up to 3 weeks per year.
    • Paid public holidays—8 days per calendar year.
    • And a host of other benefits that make coming to work a pleasing experience!

    Our Redmond team has the culture of a start-up with the security of a well-established international company on a rapid growth trajectory.

  • Business Operations Associate

    The Business Operations Associate is responsible for providing business management support. An ideal candidate will have strong planning, organizational and time management skills as well as the ability to work independently or as part of a team.

    Job Responsibilities:

    Operations Support (60%)

    • Coordinates logistics for business group meetings/events.
    • Assists with procurement of goods and services including computer equipment, office and kitchen supplies.
    • Answers incoming calls and questions and/or direct calls to appropriate department.
    • Assists visitors/deliveries at the front desk.

    Vendor/Client Support (20%)

    • Supports accounts receivable and/or accounts payable.
    • Manages administrative vendor accounts.
    • Searches out new vendors that add value to the company.
    • Processes and pays administration department invoices.

    Executive Support (20%)

    • Provides administrative support to the CEO as well as the Human Resources team.
    • Handles logistics for company travel and meetings.
    • Completes projects and special assignments by establishing objectives, determining priorities, managing time, gaining cooperation of others, monitoring progress, problem-solving and making adjustments to plans.
    • Arranges company outings and other morale events.
    • Provides general office support.

    Qualifications:

    • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Human Resources Management, or related fields.
    • Ability to solve complex problems.
    • Experience using Microsoft’s Office suite and ability to learn other technology as needed.
    • Energetic self-starter who loves to work in a dynamic environment.
    • Optimistic/positive mindset.
    • Strong organizational skills.
    • Strong interpersonal and communications skills including teamwork, facilitation and negotiation.
    • Ability to effectively handle ambiguous tasks and the ability to switch gears in response to circumstances.
    • High personal accountability.
    • Professional demeanor in all interactions.
    • Strong attention to detail and customer service skills.

  • Content Writer/Editor

    We’re looking for a creative, hardworking individual to support the company’s content and social media initiatives. The Editor will research, write, and proofread executive communications and client materials. The Editor will also work directly with the CEO and engineering team on content projects with a company-wide impact.

    Projects include:

    • Case studies
    • Best practice guides
    • Blogs
    • Infographics
    • Explainer videos
    • Social media posts
    • Ad Hoc editing tasks

    The ideal candidate will be a social media enthusiast, adept at navigating shifting priorities, and team oriented. Must have a proven track record of generating fresh content aligned to an organization’s brand voice.

    Social media responsibilities include ownership of social creative writing, calendar execution, and community engagement across the company’s social media accounts. This role will also assist the editorial and sales teams with campaigns, concepting, project management, strategic initiatives, and other tasks as business needs evolve.

    The person in this role will be one of three writers and will work out of MAQ Software's office in Redmond, Washington.

    Key Responsibilities:

    • Research topics to verify information and participate in meetings to gather relevant background. Factcheck to aid in the preliminary writing stages.
    • Work with the CEO to develop and uphold processes for requesting, creating, editing, and publishing content. Help forecast projects and troubleshoot workflow issues.
    • Provide developmental, substantive, or copy editing for materials produced by the CEO and other work units across the organization. Ensure that content is clear and compelling, is appropriate for the target audience, and reflects the company’s tone and quality standards.
    • Serve as an editor/content writer or substantive editor for print materials, digital content, and other collateral. Identify materials related to managerial and business success.
    • Build and manage the company’s presence in our social media accounts, including Glassdoor, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Quora, and LinkedIn.
    • Post content that is clear and compelling, appropriate for the target audience, and reflects the company’s tone and quality standards.
    • Work with senior management to develop the company’s narrative across various social media platforms.
    • Monitor and engage in relevant social discussions about our company, competitors, and/or industry.
    • Build expertise on best practices and strategies in social media.
    • Track, measure, and analyze all initiatives to determine social media ROI.
    • Manage paid social execution, audience building, reporting, and related activities.
    • Identify and engage with emerging social networks.
    • Other duties as assigned.

    Minimum Requirements:

    • Bachelor's degree in English, business, communications, journalism, or related field required.
    • One year of experience in communications and editing.
    • One year of experience in digital marketing and social media, with proven experience developing social media content.
    • Understanding of entrepreneurial companies, business management principles, and avid reader of business books.
    • Highly collaborative orientation. Aptitude for working with colleagues across a range of seniority levels and areas of subject matter expertise.
    • Experience managing multiple projects and competing timelines.
    • Ability to respond to shifting priorities quickly and effectively.
    • Strong interest in technology.

    Preferred Qualifications:

    • Experience working with C-level executives and IT industry professionals at all stages of the document-development process.
    • Experience writing for a newspaper or other type of business publication.
    • Personal presence on social media, with demonstrable command of multiple networks and their best practices.
    • Proficiency with social media tools like Buffer, Hootsuite, etc.
    • Computer science or engineering background.
    • Knowledge of Microsoft cloud, data, and analytics stacks.
