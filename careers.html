A little about us

At MAQ Software, we exist to build great software. With expertise in Microsoft technologies, our teams deliver solutions in data visualization, ML/AI, modern apps, and more to Fortune 500 and Global 2000 customers. Our core values in continuous learning, continuous delivery, and continuous improvement means we are always on the hunt for new ideas and technologies that offer previously unknown opportunities.

We are Agile

As an Agile company, we believe in daily delivery and continuous improvement. Results happen fast.

We are always learning

We operate with a learn-it-first mindset and encourage our teams to regularly attend industry-relevant courses and seminars.

We work with the latest technologies

Our teams work with a varied mix of the latest technologies. The work is exciting and challenging.

We are a flat org

We empower team members to take ownership and accountability from day one. You'll be exposed to all parts of project management.
